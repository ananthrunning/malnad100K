What he is attempting
A hundred kilometres through the coffee hills of South India

The Malnad Ultra 100K is one of India's most demanding trail running events, held in the Western Ghats near Chikmagalur. The course covers 100 km of mountain trail in a single push — climbing and descending 3,410 metres in total, roughly equivalent to running up and down a 340-storey building on forest paths, in the dark, over 21 hours. At 47, Ananth is attempting his first hundred kilometre race after completing the 50K edition in November 2025, which qualified him for this race.

The Malnad Ultra traces ancient paths through the coffee and spice estates of Shivamogga district — dense forest, river crossings, and unrelenting climbs through one of India's richest biodiversity zones. The race runs as two loops of the same 50 km course; the first must be completed by 4:30 PM before the second begins in the dark. Ananth qualified for the 100K by completing the 50K edition in November 2025.
